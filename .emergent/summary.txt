<product_requirements>
The application is a multi-agency accounting system for Sanhaja Travel Agencies, localized for Algeria.
**Initial Core Requirements:**
1.  **Authentication**: Role-based access (General Manager, General Accountant, Agency Staff) with hierarchical permissions, later enhanced with Google Authentication.
2.  **Database**: Isolation of all entities (Agencies, Users, Clients, Suppliers, Bookings, Invoices, Payments, Cashboxes, JournalEntries, ChartOfAccounts, Session, DailyReport) per agency.
3.  **CRUD Interfaces**: For all entities, with filters and search functionality.
4.  **Key Screens**: Dashboard, invoice creation, payment recording, comprehensive reports (daily/monthly sales, aging, journal, trial balance).
5.  **Multi-agency View**: GM and GA can view/filter all agency data, while staff see only their agency's.
6.  **Bilingual UI**: Arabic/French with language selector and professional navigation.
7.  **Notifications**: Invoice due, low cashbox, backup/restore failure alerts.
8.  **System Management**: User management and daily reports with approval workflow.

**New Requirements & Enhancements during this Trajectory:**
1.  **Daily Operations Management**: Include all daily agency work (services, sales, revenues), provide totals, and filter capabilities. Some services have fixed prices, modifiable by staff with justified reductions. Staff cannot change receipt details/date after accountant approval (GM and GA roles for approval).
2.  **Variable Pricing Services**: Add a category for Variable Services allowing agency staff to define custom prices for sales not yet officially published by the accountant.
3.  **Professional PDF Printing**: Implement professional PDF generation for invoices, receipts, daily reports, and operations. PDFs must include agency logo, address, and electronic signature of the staff member.
4.  **Receipt Preview Modal**: A modal to preview receipts with comprehensive information including agency details (address, phone), service type, payment method, amount paid/remaining, and the electronic signature of the initiating staff.
5.  **Agency-specific Settings**: Functionality to add and modify detailed information (contact numbers, addresses, registration details) for each agency.
</product_requirements>
